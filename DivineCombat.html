Divine Combat


A Top-Down Tactical Fantasy Role-Playing Game. Pick from one of several Deities with their own unique powers, skills, and Followers, and battle competing Deities, friend and foe alike, to ascend to a higher plane of existence.

My Contributions

AI

The AI will move closer to the player units determining who they will attack based on weapon advantage. If they don't have an advantage, they will determine whether the player unit has low health, if no other factors apply, they will move to the closest unit if the other

Deity Abilities

Each deity adds a passive ability as well as one active ability the player can use in battle. Passive abilities boost certain stats depending on the one you choose. The active abilities are used during battle.

Save System

This is a stat heavy game and its important that all stat upgrades move between battle. I made sure all information can be moved between scenes.
